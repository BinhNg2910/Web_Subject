Student Accessibility and Inclusion (Disability Services)

The Student Accessibility and Inclusion (SA&I) team are a team of allied health professionals who assist students experiencing temporary or permanent disability and/or medical condition/s to engage in study in the same way as a student without disability.

Violence, abuse or harassment support

Discussing and disclosing instances of violence, abuse or harassment can be traumatic, uncomfortable and difficult. UOW has adopted a support-first model that works to ensure that you are supported on your own terms. We want you to know that it is not your fault and you are not alone.
